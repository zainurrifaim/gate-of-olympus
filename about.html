About This Project

Our Mission

Gate of Olympus was created as an educational tool to raise awareness about the mechanics of slot machines and the risks associated with gambling. By simulating a real slot experience and revealing how the odds are manipulated, we hope to empower players with knowledge and promote responsible gaming habits.

Get Help

Gambling can be addictive. If you or someone you know is struggling, please don't hesitate to seek help. There are many free and confidential resources available.
